1.What is DevOps?
Devops Combines Development and Operations to increase the efficiency,speed, and security of software development and delivery compared to traditional processes.

2.What is automation?
Automation is the use of technology to perform task with reduced human assistance.

3.What is Scaling?
Scaling is the process of expanding and shrinking your systems and practices as you need them.

4.What is Infrastructure?
Infrastructure is the foundation or framework that supports a system or organization.

5.Why DevOps is important?
DevOps is important because its a software development and operations approach that enables faster development of new products and easier maintenance of existing deployments.
